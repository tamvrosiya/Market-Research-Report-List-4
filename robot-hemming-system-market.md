<p><p>Robot hemming systems can be categorized into two main types: single-station and multi-station systems. Single-station type systems are designed to perform hemming processes on one panel at a time, offering flexibility and adaptability for smaller production lines. On the other hand, multi-station type systems are capable of performing hemming processes on multiple panels simultaneously, making them ideal for high-volume production lines where efficiency and speed are critical. Each type of system caters to different production needs and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
