PCR equipment amplifies and detects DNA sequences, immunoassay systems detect antibodies or antigens, and hybrid systems combine multiple techniques for analysis. NMR systems use magnetic fields to analyze molecular structures, while flow cytometry systems analyze cells based on their physical and chemical properties. Other types of equipment include microarrays, biosensors, and ELISA systems.</p></p>
