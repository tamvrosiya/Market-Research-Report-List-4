TGHA tubes are designed for graphite furnace atomic absorption spectrometry with higher temperature capabilities, while HGA tubes are used for both flame and graphite furnace atomic absorption spectrometry. Both types of tubes play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and reliable sample analysis in various industries such as environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture.</p></p>
