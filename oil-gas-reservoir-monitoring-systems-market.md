<p><p>Oil & Gas Reservoir Monitoring Systems are categorized into different types based on the technology used. Electrical Downhole Monitoring Systems involve measuring parameters such as pressure, temperature, and flow using electrical sensors. Fiber Optic Downhole Monitoring Systems use fiber optic cables to transmit data from downhole sensors to the surface. Wireless Downhole Communication Monitoring Systems utilize wireless technology to transmit real-time data from downhole sensors. Other types of monitoring systems may include acoustic monitoring, passive seismic monitoring, or electromagnetic monitoring to gather information about reservoir conditions.</p></p>
