<p><p>Ultrasound transducer probes come in various types designed for different imaging applications. Linear type probes are used for musculoskeletal and vascular imaging, while convex type probes are ideal for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Phased array type probes are used for cardiology and vascular imaging. Endocavitary type probes are designed for pelvic and transvaginal imaging. Other types of probes cater to specific specialties such as ophthalmology and small parts imaging. Each type offers unique features and advantages for specialized ultrasound imaging needs.</p></p>
