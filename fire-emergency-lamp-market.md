<p><p>Centralized Control Type fire emergency lamps are connected to a centralized control panel that allows for coordinated monitoring and activation in case of a fire emergency. This type is commonly used in larger buildings and commercial spaces where a centralized system is preferred for ease of management. </p><p>Non-centralized Control Type fire emergency lamps operate independently without the need for a centralized control panel. Each lamp can be individually activated in case of emergency, making this type suitable for smaller buildings or residential spaces where a simpler and more cost-effective solution is preferred.</p></p>
