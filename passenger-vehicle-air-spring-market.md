<p><p>Passenger vehicle air springs come in various types, including convoluted type, sleeve type, and rolling lobe type. Convoluted type air springs are shaped like a bellows, providing a more versatile and flexible solution for various applications. Sleeve type air springs feature a cylindrical design, offering high load-bearing capacity and durability. Rolling lobe type air springs have a rolling lobe construction, allowing for smooth and comfortable riding experience. Each type serves different purposes and caters to specific needs in the passenger vehicle air spring market.</p></p>
