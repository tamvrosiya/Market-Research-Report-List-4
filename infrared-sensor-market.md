<p><p>Infrared sensors are used in a variety of applications and come in different types. Thermopile infrared sensors detect infrared radiation through the use of thermopiles, which generate a voltage when exposed to heat. Pyroelectric infrared sensors, on the other hand, use pyroelectric materials to generate a voltage when their temperature changes due to absorbed infrared radiation. Both types of sensors are widely used in applications such as temperature measurement, motion detection, and security systems.</p></p>
