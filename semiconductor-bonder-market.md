<p><strong>The Semiconductor Bonder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Bonder</li><li>Die Bonder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor bonder market can be segmented into two main types: wire bonder and die bonder. Wire bonder is used to connect semiconductor devices to package leads using thin wires, while die bonder is used to accurately place and bond individual semiconductor dies onto a substrate. Both types of bonders play crucial roles in the semiconductor manufacturing process by ensuring reliable connections and precise positioning of components. The wire bonder market focuses on wire bonding applications, while the die bonder market specializes in die attachment processes.</p></p>
