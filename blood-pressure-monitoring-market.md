<p><p>Automated BP Monitors are devices that automatically inflate a cuff and record blood pressure measurements. They are commonly used in healthcare settings and provide quick and accurate readings. Ambulatory BP Monitors are portable devices that patients can wear throughout the day to monitor their blood pressure continuously. They are useful for diagnosing conditions like white coat hypertension and nocturnal hypertension. Both types of monitors are important tools in managing and monitoring blood pressure levels for individuals with hypertension or other cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
