<p><p>Wireless remote control vehicle barrier gates come in two main types: straight and crank. Straight barrier gates are designed with a simple arm that raises and lowers vertically to allow or restrict vehicle access. Crank barrier gates, on the other hand, utilize a rotating arm that swings in and out to control traffic flow. Both types of barrier gates are operated wirelessly using remote control devices, offering convenience and efficiency for managing vehicle access in various settings such as parking lots, residential communities, and commercial properties.</p></p>
