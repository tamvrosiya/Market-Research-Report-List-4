<p><p>Lithium-ion battery materials market can be categorized into two main types: cathode materials and anode materials. Cathode materials are responsible for the flow of lithium ions from the cathode to the anode during battery charging and discharging processes. They play a crucial role in determining the battery's energy density and overall performance. On the other hand, anode materials are responsible for storing lithium ions during battery charging. Both cathode and anode materials are essential components in the production of high-quality lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
