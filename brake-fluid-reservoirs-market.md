<p><p>Brake fluid reservoirs come in two main types: those that are put on top of the brake master cylinder and those that are separate containers. Reservoirs that are placed on top of the master cylinder are integrated into the overall design, making them easier to access for maintenance. On the other hand, separate containers offer flexibility in placement and can be positioned in a variety of locations within the vehicle. Both types serve the important function of storing and supplying brake fluid to the braking system.</p></p>
