<p><p>Virtual Pipeline systems utilize mobile CNG storage units to transport compressed natural gas to remote areas without access to pipelines. Plug-and-Play CNG systems include gas storage units, air supply units, control units, and other components that allow for quick and easy installation and operation. The Gas Storage Unit stores compressed natural gas, the Air Supply Unit regulates air intake for compression, the Control Unit monitors and controls system operations, while other components may include filtration units or safety devices. These systems provide a convenient and flexible solution for natural gas transportation and distribution.</p></p>
