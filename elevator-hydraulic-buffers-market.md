<p><strong>The Elevator Hydraulic Buffers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tap</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elevator hydraulic buffers are available in two main types: tap and vertical. </p><p>Tap buffers are installed at the end of hydraulic pistons to absorb the impact of the elevator car when it reaches the bottom of the shaft. They help prevent damage to the elevator components and ensure a smooth ride for passengers.</p><p>Vertical buffers are typically installed on the top of the elevator car to cushion the impact of sudden stops or starts. They provide a similar function to tap buffers but are designed for different elevator systems.</p></p>
