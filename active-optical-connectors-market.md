<p><p>Active Optical Connectors are innovative technology solutions for high-speed data transmission. Simplex Active Optical Connectors are single-channel connectors that transmit data in one direction, while Duplex Active Optical Connectors are dual-channel connectors that enable bidirectional transmission. Simplex connectors are ideal for applications where data flow is unidirectional, such as in point-to-point communication systems. On the other hand, Duplex connectors are suitable for applications requiring simultaneous transmission and reception of data, such as in networking and telecommunications systems.</p></p>
