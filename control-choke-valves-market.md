<p><p>Control choke valves are used to regulate the flow of fluids in industrial processes. There are two main types of control choke valves: fixed choke valves and adjustable choke valves. Fixed choke valves have a predetermined opening size that cannot be changed, while adjustable choke valves allow for the adjustment of the opening size depending on the flow requirements. Both types of choke valves play a crucial role in controlling the flow rate and pressure of fluids in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and power generation.</p></p>
