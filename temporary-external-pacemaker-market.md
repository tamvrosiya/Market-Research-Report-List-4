<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Dual Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Triple Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Temporary External Pacemaker market consists of three main types: Single Chamber, Dual Chamber, and Triple Chamber. Single Chamber pacemakers deliver electrical impulses to one chamber of the heart, while Dual Chamber pacemakers deliver impulses to two chambers. Triple Chamber pacemakers deliver impulses to the atria and both ventricles of the heart. Each type of pacemaker is used temporarily to regulate the heart's rhythm in patients who require short-term pacing support, such as during surgery or in cases of heart block.</p></p>
