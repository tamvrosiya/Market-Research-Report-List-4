<p><p>The Static Smart Meter market can be categorized into two main types: Direct Current Meters and Alternating Current Meters. Direct Current Meters are designed to measure and monitor the flow of current in a straight line, commonly used in electronic devices and solar panels. Alternating Current Meters, on the other hand, are used to measure the flow of current that constantly changes direction, commonly found in household electrical systems. Both types of meters play a crucial role in providing accurate and real-time energy consumption data for better management and conservation.</p></p>
