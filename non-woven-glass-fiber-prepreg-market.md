<p><p>Non-woven glass fiber prepreg markets can be classified based on the binder material used in the manufacturing process. PP, PET, PE, Rayon, and Wood Pulp are common binder materials used in non-woven glass fiber prepregs. PP (polypropylene), PET (polyethylene terephthalate), and PE (polyethylene) are synthetic materials that offer durability and resistance to moisture. Rayon is a natural fiber known for its softness and breathability. Wood pulp is a cellulose-based material that provides biodegradability and sustainability to the prepreg products.</p></p>
