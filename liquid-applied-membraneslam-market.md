<p><p>Liquid Applied Membranes (LAM) are versatile waterproofing systems applied as a liquid coating that sets into a durable, seamless membrane. The market for LAM is segmented into different types based on the composition of the membrane. Bituminous LAMs are made from bitumen and offer good flexibility. Elastomeric LAMs are made from synthetic rubber polymers for enhanced stretching ability. Cementitious LAMs are made from cement-based materials for strong adhesion. Other types include hybrid LAMs that combine different materials for specific performance characteristics.</p></p>
