<p><p>Process Safety Systems market consists of various types such as Emergency Shutdown Systems (ESS), Fire and Gas (F&G) Systems, Burner Management Systems (BMS), and High-Integrity Pressure Protection Systems (HIPPS). ESS ensure rapid shutdown of operations in case of emergencies, while F&G Systems detect and respond to fires and gas leaks. BMS manage and monitor burners in industrial processes, and HIPPS prevent overpressure incidents in pipelines or vessels. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and integrity of industrial processes.</p></p>
