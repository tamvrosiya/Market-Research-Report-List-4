Standard HPLC pumps are typically used for routine applications, while UHPLC pumps are designed for higher pressure and faster separations. UHPLC pumps are capable of operating at pressures greater than 15,000 psi, allowing for more efficient and quicker analysis compared to standard HPLC pumps.</p></p>
