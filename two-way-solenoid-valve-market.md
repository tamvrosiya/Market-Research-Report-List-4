<p><strong>The Two-Way Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-acting Type</li><li>Sub-step Direct-acting Type</li><li>Pilot-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two-way solenoid valve market includes three main types: direct-acting type, sub-step direct-acting type, and pilot-type. Direct-acting type valves do not require an external power source, as they operate using the pressure of the fluid itself. Sub-step direct-acting type valves have a fast response time due to their design, which reduces the time needed for opening and closing. Pilot-type valves use a smaller solenoid to control a larger valve, allowing for higher flow rates and pressures.</p></p>
