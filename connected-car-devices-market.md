<p><p>Connected car devices have revolutionized the automotive industry with various market types. Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) technology allows cars to communicate with each other, sharing information about road conditions and potential hazards. Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) enables vehicles to connect with traffic signals, parking meters, and other infrastructure to improve safety and efficiency. Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) technology ensures the safety of pedestrians by allowing vehicles to detect their presence and avoid potential accidents. These market types collectively enhance communication and overall driving experience.</p></p>
