<p><p>Cellulose filaments market consists of two main types: Wood pulp-based and refined cotton-based. Wood pulp-based cellulose filaments are derived from wood pulp fibers and are widely used in industries such as paper, textiles, and construction due to their strength and versatility. On the other hand, refined cotton-based cellulose filaments are made from refined cotton fibers and are preferred for their softness and absorbency, making them suitable for applications in the textile and hygiene industries. Both types offer unique properties and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
