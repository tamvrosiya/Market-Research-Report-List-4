<p><p>Airway adapters are essential medical devices that connect ventilators to the patient's airway during respiratory support. There are two main types of airway adapters in the market: single channel adapters and dual channel adapters. Single channel adapters have one pathway for air to flow between the ventilator and the patient, while dual channel adapters have two separate pathways for oxygen inflow and carbon dioxide outflow. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper ventilation and oxygenation for patients in need of respiratory support.</p></p>
