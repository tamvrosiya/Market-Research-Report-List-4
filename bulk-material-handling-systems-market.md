<p><p>Bulk material handling systems market includes various types of equipment such as stackers, stackers cum reclaimers, band conveyors, bucket wheel excavators, stripping shovels, rope shovels, bucket elevators, ship loaders, and ship unloaders. Stacker equipment is used to pile bulk materials in a uniform manner, while reclaimers are used for simultaneous stacking and reclaiming operations. Band conveyors are used for horizontal and inclined transportation of bulk materials. Bucket wheel excavators are used for continuous digging and loading of bulk materials. Ship loaders and unloaders are used for efficient loading and unloading of ships with bulk materials.</p></p>
