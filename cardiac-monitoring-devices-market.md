<p><p>Cardiac monitoring devices market types include ECG method, arterial pressure method, and photoelectric method. The ECG method involves measuring the electrical activity of the heart to diagnose abnormalities. The arterial pressure method monitors blood pressure continuously to detect changes in cardiac function. The photoelectric method measures changes in blood flow and oxygen levels in the heart by shining light through the skin. These devices play a critical role in diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions, helping healthcare providers deliver appropriate treatment.</p></p>
