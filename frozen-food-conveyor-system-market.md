<p><p>Frozen food conveyor systems are classified into two main types: open conveyor systems and closed conveyor systems. Open conveyor systems are designed with minimal enclosure and are commonly used for the transportation of frozen products in a continuous flow. On the other hand, closed conveyor systems are fully enclosed and protect the frozen products from external contaminants, maintaining food safety standards. Both systems offer efficient transportation of frozen foods, but closed systems provide better protection and are ideal for sensitive food products.</p></p>
