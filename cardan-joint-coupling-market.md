<p><p>Cardan joint couplings are classified into different types based on their design and construction. Single Cardan joints consist of one universal joint while Double Cardan joints have two universal joints connected by a centering yoke. Centered and Staked joints have a centering device to maintain alignment, while other types may have unique features or designs. Each type offers specific benefits and is used in various applications depending on the requirements for flexibility, alignment, and torque transmission.</p></p>
