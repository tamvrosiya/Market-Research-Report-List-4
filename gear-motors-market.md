<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Gear Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Shaft Gear Motors</li><li>Right-Angle Shaft Gear Motors</li><li>Planetary Gear Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gear motors are classified into three main types based on the orientation of the output shaft: Parallel Shaft Gear Motors, Right-Angle Shaft Gear Motors, and Planetary Gear Motors. Parallel Shaft Gear Motors have the output shaft in line with the motor shaft, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Right-Angle Shaft Gear Motors have a 90-degree turn in the shaft, ideal for tight spaces and applications requiring torque. Planetary Gear Motors have higher efficiency and torque density due to their compact design, making them suitable for high precision applications.</p></p>
