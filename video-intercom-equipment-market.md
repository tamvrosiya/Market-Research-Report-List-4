<p><p>Video intercom equipment comes in two main types: Analog Type and IP Type. Analog Type systems use traditional analog cables to transmit audio and video signals. They are suitable for smaller installations and offer basic features. On the other hand, IP Type systems use internet protocol networks to transmit data, providing more advanced functionalities such as remote access and integration with other devices. They are ideal for larger installations and offer greater flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
