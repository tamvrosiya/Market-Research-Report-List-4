<p><ul><li>Hole Transport Layer Materials (HTL)</li><li>Electron Transport Layer Materials (ETL)</li><li>Electrode materials</li><li>Emissive Layer Materials (EML)</li><li>Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) market consists of various types of materials and components such as Hole Transport Layer Materials (HTL), Electron Transport Layer Materials (ETL), Electrode materials, Emissive Layer Materials (EML), and Substrates. HTL materials facilitate the movement of positive charges, while ETL materials facilitate the movement of negative charges. Electrode materials are used for injecting charges into the device. EML materials emit light when charges recombine. Substrates provide a base for the OLED device to be built upon.</p></p>
