<p><p>A flexible ring coupling is a type of coupling that is used to connect two shafts together in order to transmit power or torque between them. It is designed to accommodate misalignment between the two shafts, as well as to reduce vibration and shock in the system. The flexibility of the coupling is provided by a series of rubber or elastomeric rings that are housed within the coupling.</p><p>The Flexible Ring Coupling Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing demand for flexible couplings in various industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and aerospace. Flexible ring couplings offer several advantages over rigid couplings, including the ability to absorb shock and vibration, reduce noise, and extend the life of equipment.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Flexible Ring Coupling Market include the development of new materials and technologies to improve the performance and efficiency of couplings, as well as the increasing adoption of maintenance-free and low-maintenance couplings in various applications.
