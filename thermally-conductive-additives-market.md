<p><p>Thermally conductive additives are used to enhance the thermal conductivity of materials. Two main types of thermally conductive additives include thermoplastic conductive additives and thermosetting conductive additives. Thermoplastic conductive additives are added to thermoplastic materials to improve their thermal conductivity, while thermosetting conductive additives are incorporated into thermosetting materials for the same purpose. Both types of additives play a crucial role in improving heat transfer properties in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
