<p><p>Nucleic Acid Vaccine Market consists of two main types: Preventive Nucleic Acid Vaccine and Therapeutic Nucleic Acid Vaccine. Preventive Nucleic Acid Vaccines are designed to prevent viral infections by stimulating the immune system to produce antibodies before exposure to the virus. On the other hand, Therapeutic Nucleic Acid Vaccines are developed to treat existing infections by targeting specific antigens on the virus to help the immune system recognize and eliminate the virus effectively. These two types of vaccines play a crucial role in preventing and treating viral diseases.</p></p>
