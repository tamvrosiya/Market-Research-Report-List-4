<p><p>Near Infrared Spectrum Instruments are classified into three types based on their technology: Filter Type, Dispersion Type, and Fourier Transform Type. Filter Type instruments use various filters to isolate specific wavelengths of near-infrared light. Dispersion Type instruments disperse light using diffraction gratings or prisms to separate different wavelengths. Fourier Transform Type instruments utilize interferometers to measure the entire spectrum simultaneously. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different industries and research needs.</p></p>
