<p><p>Beta-Glucan nutraceuticals are available in various market types, including cereal beta-glucan, mushroom beta-glucan, yeast beta-glucan, and others. Mushroom beta-glucan is sourced from fungi such as shiitake and reishi, popular for their immune-boosting effects. Yeast beta-glucan comes from the cell walls of yeast, offering potential benefits for heart health and immune support. Other types of beta-glucan may include algae-derived or synthetic formulations.</p></p>
