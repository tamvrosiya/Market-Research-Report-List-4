<p><p>High Performance Servo Actuators are classified into two main types based on their power source: AC and DC. AC servo actuators are powered by alternating current electricity, providing a continuous power supply for efficient and precise operation. On the other hand, DC servo actuators are powered by direct current electricity, offering fast response times and high torque output. Both types are popular choices in various industries for their superior performance capabilities and reliability.</p></p>
