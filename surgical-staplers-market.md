<p><p>Surgical staplers are divided into two main categories: disposable staplers and reusable staplers. Disposable staplers are designed for single use in surgical procedures, reducing the risk of cross-contamination and infection. They are convenient, cost-effective, and ensure consistent stapling performance. Reusable staplers, on the other hand, are sterilizable and used multiple times, making them more environmentally friendly and cost-efficient in the long run. Both types of staplers offer their own unique benefits and are widely used in various surgical settings.</p></p>
