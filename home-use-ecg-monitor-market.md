<p><strong>The Home Use ECG Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG Monitor</li><li>Holter ECG Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resting ECG monitors are designed for daily monitoring of heart activity at home. They are typically used to track regular heart rhythms and detect any abnormalities while resting. On the other hand, Holter ECG monitors are portable devices that can provide continuous monitoring of heart activity for an extended period, usually 24 to 48 hours. They are useful for capturing irregular heart rhythms that may not show up during a resting ECG. Both types of ECG monitors are valuable tools for monitoring heart health outside of a clinical setting.</p></p>
