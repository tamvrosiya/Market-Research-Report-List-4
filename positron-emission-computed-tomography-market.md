<p><p>Positron emission computed tomography (PET) market consists of direct imaging, indirect imaging, and alternative imaging. Direct imaging involves using positron-emitting radiopharmaceuticals for PET imaging. Indirect imaging involves using positron-emitting radiopharmaceuticals in combination with other imaging modalities, such as CT or MRI. Alternative imaging refers to non-traditional applications of PET technology, such as dynamic imaging or metabolic imaging. These different types cater to various medical needs and contribute to the growth and advancement of the PET market.</p></p>
