<p><strong>The Lightning Protection System(LPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Lightning Protection System</li><li>Non-Conventional Lightning Protection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lightning Protection System (LPS) market includes two main types - Conventional and Non-Conventional systems. Conventional LPS consists of rods, wires, and grounding systems to redirect lightning strikes to the ground, protecting buildings and structures. Non-Conventional LPS utilizes advanced technologies such as early streamer emission (ESE) and charge transfer systems to offer enhanced protection. These systems provide a more proactive approach to lightning protection, detecting and diverting lightning strikes before they can cause damage. Both types play a crucial role in safeguarding lives and property from the destructive power of lightning.</p></p>
