<p><strong>The SMT AXI Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline</li><li>Offline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SMT AXI Inspection Equipment market can be classified into two types: Inline and Offline. Inline AXI inspection equipment is integrated into the production line and inspects the PCBs as they move through the manufacturing process. This real-time inspection helps detect defects early on and ensures quality control. Offline AXI inspection equipment, on the other hand, is used separately from the production line to inspect finished PCBs. It allows for thorough and detailed analysis of PCBs to identify any potential defects or issues that may have been missed during inline inspection.</p></p>
