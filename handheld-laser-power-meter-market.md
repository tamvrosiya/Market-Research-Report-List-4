There are two main types of Handheld Laser Power Meters - Photoelectric Type and Thermoelectric Type. The Photoelectric Type measures power by converting light into electrical signals, while the Thermoelectric Type measures power by converting heat generated by the laser into electrical signals. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of users in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and specific application requirements within the laser industry.</p></p>
