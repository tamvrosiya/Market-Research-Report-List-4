<p><strong>The Medical Housings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Forming</li><li>Pressure Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical housings market consists of various types of technologies, including vacuum forming and pressure forming. Vacuum forming involves heating a plastic sheet and sucking it onto a mold using a vacuum, creating a seamless housing. Pressure forming, on the other hand, uses pressure to force the plastic sheet onto the mold, resulting in a more precise and higher-quality product. Both technologies are widely used in the medical industry for creating durable and precise housings for medical equipment.</p></p>
