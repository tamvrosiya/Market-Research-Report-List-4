<p><p>Military servo motor market consists of two main types: DC servo motors and AC servo motors. DC servo motors are known for their high torque to inertia ratio, making them a popular choice for military applications where precise control and high acceleration are required. On the other hand, AC servo motors are preferred for their ability to operate at high speeds and provide smooth motion control. Both types of servo motors are essential components in military equipment such as radar systems, missile guidance, and surveillance systems.</p></p>
