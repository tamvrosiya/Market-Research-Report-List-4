The three main types include hydrosurgical debridement devices, which use a pressurized water jet to clean the wound, mechanical debridement pads, which physically remove debris from the wound surface, and low frequency ultrasound devices, which use sound waves to break down and remove dead tissue. Each type of device offers a different approach to wound debridement, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
