<p><p>The Insulin API market can be broadly categorized into two main types: Regular Human Insulin and Insulin Analogue. Regular Human Insulin is a synthetic version of the hormone produced by the pancreas, which helps regulate blood sugar levels. Insulin Analogue, on the other hand, is a modified form of insulin that is designed to mimic the body's natural insulin production more closely. Both types of insulin are vital for managing diabetes and are essential components of the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
