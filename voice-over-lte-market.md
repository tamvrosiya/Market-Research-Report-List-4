<p><strong>The Voice over LTE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frequency Division Duplexing (FDD)</li><li>Time Division Duplexing (TDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voice over LTE (VoLTE) market includes two types of technologies - Frequency Division Duplexing (FDD) and Time Division Duplexing (TDD). FDD uses separate frequencies for the uplink and downlink transmissions, allowing for simultaneous two-way communication. TDD, on the other hand, uses the same frequency for both uplink and downlink transmissions, but in different time slots. Both FDD and TDD are used in the VoLTE market to provide high-quality voice calls and enhanced mobile communication services.</p></p>
