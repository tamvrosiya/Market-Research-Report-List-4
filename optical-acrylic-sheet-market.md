<p><p>Optical Acrylic Sheet is available in two main types: Extruded Acrylic Sheet and Cast Acrylic Sheet. Extruded Acrylic Sheets are made by continuously pushing acrylic through a mold, resulting in a more affordable option with limited color options. Cast Acrylic Sheets are made by pouring liquid acrylic into a mold and allowing it to set, producing a higher quality product with more color options but at a higher cost. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on their properties and cost.</p></p>
