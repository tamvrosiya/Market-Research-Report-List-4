<p><p>Medical/Diagnostic Imaging Market types include X-ray, Computed Tomography (CT), Ultrasound equipment, MRI equipment, and Nuclear imaging. X-ray imaging uses electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the body's internal structures. CT scans combine multiple X-ray images to create detailed cross-sectional images. Ultrasound equipment uses high-frequency sound waves to create images of internal organs. MRI equipment uses magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of soft tissues. Nuclear imaging involves the use of small amounts of radioactive materials to diagnose and treat diseases.</p></p>
