<p><p>Ticket dispensers are commonly found in two main market types: manual and automatic. Manual ticket dispensers require users to physically pull or tear a ticket from a roll, often found in places like delis or raffles. Automatic ticket dispensers, on the other hand, operate electronically and automatically dispense tickets when a button is pressed or a sensor is activated. These are commonly used in places like parking garages, movie theaters, and amusement parks for quick and efficient ticket distribution.</p></p>
