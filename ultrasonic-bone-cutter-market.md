<p><p>Ultrasonic bone cutters are specialized medical devices used in surgical procedures. They come in two main types: Straight Tip and Angled Tip. Straight Tip bone cutters are designed for precise and straight cuts, ideal for procedures requiring accuracy and control. On the other hand, Angled Tip bone cutters are used for procedures where access may be limited or where angled cuts are necessary. Both types of bone cutters utilize ultrasonic technology to provide efficient cutting while minimizing heat generation and tissue damage.</p></p>
