<p><p>Medical loupes are widely used in the healthcare industry to provide magnification and improved vision during medical procedures. There are four main types of medical loupes: Through The Lens Loupe (TTL), Flip Up Loupe, Galilean Loupe, and Prismatic Loupe. TTL loupes have integrated lenses, Flip Up loupes can be moved out of the field of vision, Galilean loupes use multiple lenses for magnification, and Prismatic loupes use prisms to enhance vision. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different medical specialties.</p></p>
