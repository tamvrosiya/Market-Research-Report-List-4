<p><ul><li>Alternate Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer</li><li>Random Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Styrene Maleic Anhydride (SMA) market includes two main types: Alternate Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer and Random Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer. Alternate SMA copolymers have a consistent alternating structure of styrene and maleic anhydride units, providing unique properties such as high transparency and thermal stability. On the other hand, Random SMA copolymers have a random arrangement of styrene and maleic anhydride units, offering different properties like improved adhesion and compatibility with other materials. Both types cater to various industries such as automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
