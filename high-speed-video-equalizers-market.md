<p><ul><li>Semi-parametric Equalizers</li><li>Fully-parametric Equalizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Speed Video Equalizers are a type of electronic device used to adjust the frequency response of video signals. Semi-parametric Equalizers allow users to adjust the center frequency and bandwidth of specific frequency bands, providing some flexibility in shaping the sound. Fully-parametric Equalizers offer even more control, allowing users to adjust the center frequency, bandwidth, and gain of each frequency band independently. Both types of equalizers are commonly used in professional audio production and video editing to achieve desired sound quality and clarity.</p></p>
