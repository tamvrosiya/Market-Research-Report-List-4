<p><p>Silicone Rubber Waterproof Coating can be categorized into two main types: Liquid Silicone Rubber Waterproof Coating and Dry Silicone Rubber Waterproof Coating. Liquid silicone coatings are applied as a liquid that then cures to form a seamless, waterproof barrier. Dry silicone coatings typically come in sheet or roll form and are applied by adhering them directly to the surface. Both types of coatings offer excellent waterproofing properties and are commonly used in various industries for protecting surfaces from water damage.</p></p>
