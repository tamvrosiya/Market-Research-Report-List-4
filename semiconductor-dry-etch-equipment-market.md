<p><p>Semiconductor dry etch equipment market includes various types such as dielectric etching, silicon etching, metal etching, and others. Dielectric etching involves the removal of dielectric materials, while silicon etching is used to etch patterns into silicon substrates. Metal etching is the process of selectively removing metal layers. Other types may include compound semiconductors etching, plasma etching, and reactive-ion etching. Each type of etching equipment plays a critical role in the semiconductor manufacturing process, contributing to the production of advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
