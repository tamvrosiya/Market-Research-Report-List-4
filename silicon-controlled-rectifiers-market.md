<p><p>Silicon Controlled Rectifiers (SCR) market types include unidirectional SCR and bidirectional SCR. Unidirectional SCRs conduct current in one direction only, making them suitable for applications where current flows in a single direction. On the other hand, bidirectional SCRs allow current to flow in both directions, making them useful for bidirectional power control applications. Both types of SCRs are widely used in various industries such as power electronics, motor control, and lighting control for their reliability and efficiency in controlling power flow.</p></p>
