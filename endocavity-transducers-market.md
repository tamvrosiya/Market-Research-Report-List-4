<p><p>Endocavity transducers are used in ultrasound imaging and come in various market types, including curvilinear, phased array, endocavity, and linear. Curvilinear transducers are used for imaging deeper structures and organs, while phased array transducers offer improved resolution and can be steered electronically. Endocavity transducers are specifically designed for use inside body cavities, providing detailed images in confined spaces. Linear transducers produce high-resolution images of superficial structures. Each type offers unique capabilities suited for different clinical applications in ultrasound imaging.</p></p>
