<p><strong>The Laser Energy Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyroelectric</li><li>Photodiode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Energy Detectors market consists of two main types: Pyroelectric and Photodiode detectors. Pyroelectric detectors are based on the principle that the absorption of laser energy by a material causes a change in its temperature, resulting in a measurable electrical signal. On the other hand, Photodiode detectors rely on the conversion of incident laser energy into an electrical current. Both types of detectors are commonly used in various applications such as laser power monitoring, environmental monitoring, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
