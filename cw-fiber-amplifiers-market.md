<p><p>CW fiber amplifiers are commonly classified into two main types: broadband and single frequency. Broadband amplifiers have a wide wavelength range, allowing them to amplify multiple signals simultaneously. They are versatile and ideal for applications that require amplification of various wavelengths. On the other hand, single frequency amplifiers are specifically designed to amplify a single narrow wavelength range. They offer high signal purity and are suitable for applications that demand precise amplification of a single frequency signal.</p></p>
