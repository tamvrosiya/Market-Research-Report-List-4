<p><p>Point of Care Ultrasound (POCUS) systems are classified into two main types: Trolley Based and Handheld. Trolley Based systems are larger and typically used in hospitals or clinical settings where mobility is not a concern. These systems provide advanced imaging capabilities and are equipped with a variety of probes for different imaging needs. On the other hand, Handheld systems are smaller, portable devices that are easy to carry around and are ideal for use in emergency situations or at the patient's bedside for quick assessments.</p></p>
