<p><p>Electrical Ablators come in various types, including Argon Plasma/Beam Coagulators and Irreversible Electroporation Ablators. Argon Plasma/Beam Coagulators utilize high-frequency electrical current to produce ionized argon gas for precise tissue destruction in medical procedures. Irreversible Electroporation Ablators, on the other hand, use short, high-voltage electrical pulses to create nanopores in cell membranes, leading to cell death. Both technologies are used in medical treatments to selectively destroy unwanted tissues.</p></p>
