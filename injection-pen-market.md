<p><p>Injection pens can be categorized into two main types: Disposable Injection Pens and Reusable Injection Pens. Disposable pens are designed for one-time use and are pre-filled with a specific dose of medication. Once the medication is used up, the pen is discarded. On the other hand, reusable pens can be refilled with medication multiple times, making them more environmentally friendly and cost-effective in the long run. Both types of pens are commonly used for self-administration of medications such as insulin.</p></p>
