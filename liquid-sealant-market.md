<p><p>Liquid sealants are available in various types based on the composition of the material used. Resin type sealants are made from synthetic resins that provide strong adhesion and flexibility. Rubber type sealants use rubber compounds to create a durable and weather-resistant seal. Oil modified type sealants contain oil-based materials that offer excellent water resistance. Natural polymer type sealants are made from natural polymers such as cellulose or starch, providing an environmentally-friendly option for sealing applications. Each type has unique properties suited for different sealing requirements.</p></p>
