<p><p>Hospital Real-time Location Systems (RTLS) market includes various technologies such as RFID, Wi-Fi, Ultrasound, Infrared, and others. RFID technology uses radio frequency to track assets or individuals within a hospital setting. Wi-Fi technology leverages existing Wi-Fi networks to provide location data. Ultrasound technology uses sound waves to determine the location of objects. Infrared technology utilizes infrared signals for tracking purposes. Other technologies in the market may include Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and Zigbee. These technologies help hospitals improve asset tracking, patient safety, and workflow efficiency.</p></p>
