<p><p>Infant Nasal CPAP therapy utilizes different market types such as High Flow Nasal Cannula (HFNC) and Mechanical CPAP. HFNC provides humidified air at high flow rates, maintaining constant positive pressure in the airway. This type is useful for infants with mild respiratory distress. Mechanical CPAP delivers a set pressure to keep the airway open, commonly used for more severe cases. Both market types are essential in providing respiratory support to infants with respiratory issues, improving oxygenation and reducing the need for intubation.</p></p>
