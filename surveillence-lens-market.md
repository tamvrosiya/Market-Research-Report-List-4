They have a 1-inch diameter thread and provide flexibility in adjusting the focal length. On the other hand, CS-Mount lenses are smaller in size and have a 1/2-inch diameter thread, making them ideal for compact surveillance cameras. They are commonly used in applications where space is limited.</p></p>
