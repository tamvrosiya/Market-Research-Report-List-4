<p><p>Radio Walkie-Talkie market consists of two main types: Simplex and Duplex. Simplex Radio Walkie-Talkies are basic devices that allow communication in one direction at a time, similar to a traditional walkie-talkie. On the other hand, Duplex Radio Walkie-Talkies enable two-way communication, allowing users to talk and listen simultaneously. The market for Simplex devices is more affordable and suitable for short-range communication, while Duplex devices are more advanced and often used in professional settings requiring real-time, two-way communication.</p></p>
