There are two main types of retarders in the market: electric retarders and hydraulic retarders. Electric retarders use electromagnetic resistance to slow down a vehicle, while hydraulic retarders use hydraulic fluid to generate braking force. Both types offer efficient braking performance and are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles and industrial machinery for enhanced safety and control.</p></p>
