<p><p>Conventional Pump Controller refers to traditional pump control systems that are manually operated and require physical presence for monitoring and adjustments. On the other hand, Mobile/Remote Pump Controller allows users to monitor and control pump operations remotely via mobile devices or computers. This type of controller provides convenience and flexibility as it enables users to manage pumps from anywhere, improving efficiency and reducing the need for on-site supervision. Both types of controllers are used in various applications such as water supply, irrigation, and industrial processes.</p></p>
