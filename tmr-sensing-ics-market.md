<p><p>TMR sensing ICs are categorized into two main types: Analog TMR sensing ICs and Digital TMR sensing ICs. Analog TMR sensing ICs provide continuous output signals that vary based on the magnetic field strength, making them suitable for applications requiring precise measurements. On the other hand, Digital TMR sensing ICs convert magnetic field strength into digital output signals, making them ideal for applications where binary information is sufficient. Both types of TMR sensing ICs offer unique advantages and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
