<p><p>Video Surveillance as a Service (VSaaS) market includes three main types: Hosted, Managed, and Hybrid. Hosted VSaaS involves storing video footage on a remote server, accessed through the internet. Managed VSaaS includes installation and maintenance services provided by a third-party provider. Hybrid VSaaS combines elements of both hosted and managed services, allowing for video storage on both local and remote servers. Each type offers different levels of convenience, customization, and security, catering to the diverse needs of businesses and consumers in the surveillance industry.</p></p>
