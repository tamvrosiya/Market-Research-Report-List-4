<p><p>Industrial propionaldehyde is produced through various methods including Oxo process, Propylene Oxide Isomerization Method, Raper One-step Synthesis, and Propionaldehyde Liquid Phase Oxidation. The Oxo process involves hydroformylation of propylene with synthesis gas. The Propylene Oxide Isomerization Method converts propylene oxide into propionaldehyde. Raper One-step Synthesis utilizes ethanol as a starting material to produce propionaldehyde. Propionaldehyde Liquid Phase Oxidation involves the oxidation of ethylene in the liquid phase. These methods cater to different market needs and production requirements.</p></p>
