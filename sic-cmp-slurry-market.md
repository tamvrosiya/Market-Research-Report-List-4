<p><p>SiC CMP Slurry is divided into two market types: Bulk Removal Slurry and Fine Polish Slurry. Bulk Removal Slurry is used in the early stages of the semiconductor manufacturing process to rapidly remove excess material from the wafer's surface. On the other hand, Fine Polish Slurry is employed in the final stages to achieve a smooth and mirror-like finish on the wafer. Both types of slurry play crucial roles in ensuring the quality and precision of semiconductor production.</p></p>
