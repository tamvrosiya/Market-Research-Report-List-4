<p><p>Distributed optical sensing systems have three main market types: Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS), Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), and Distributed Vibration Sensing (DVS). </p><p>DTS monitors temperature changes along a fiber optic cable for various applications such as oil and gas pipelines. DAS detects acoustic signals to monitor activity in areas like perimeter security or pipeline leakage. DVS measures vibrations to monitor structural health in applications like bridges or buildings. These systems provide real-time data for enhanced monitoring and safety.</p></p>
