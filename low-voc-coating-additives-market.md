<p><p>Low-VOC coating additives market is segmented into various types based on their functions. Rheology modifiers are additives that help in controlling the flow and consistency of the coating. Defoamers are used to eliminate foam formation in the coating during application. Dispersants help in dispersing solid particles evenly in the liquid coating. Wetting agents improve the spreading and adhesion of the coating on the surface. Other types of additives may include anti-settling agents, anti-skinning agents, and crosslinkers, which have specific functions in enhancing the performance of low-VOC coatings.</p></p>
