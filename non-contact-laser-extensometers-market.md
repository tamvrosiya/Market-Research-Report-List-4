<p><p>Non-Contact Laser Extensometers are categorized into two main types, namely Built-in Laser Extensometers and Frame Laser Extensometers. Built-in Laser Extensometers are integrated into the testing equipment itself, providing accurate measurements during testing processes. On the other hand, Frame Laser Extensometers are separate devices attached to the frame of the testing equipment, allowing for more flexibility in measurement positioning. Both types are essential tools in various industries such as research, manufacturing, and quality control for ensuring precise and reliable measurements.</p></p>
