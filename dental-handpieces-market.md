<p><p>Dental handpieces are categorized into three main types: Air-Driven Handpiece, Electric Handpiece, and Hybrid Air-Electric Handpiece. Air-Driven Handpieces use compressed air to drive the handpiece, making them lightweight and affordable. Electric Handpieces operate using an electric motor, providing higher torque and consistent power. Hybrid Air-Electric Handpieces combine features of both air-driven and electric handpieces, offering versatility and efficiency. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of dental professionals, allowing for a range of options in the market.</p></p>
