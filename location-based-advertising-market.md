<p><p>Location-based advertising can be classified into two main types: push and pull marketing. Push marketing involves businesses sending promotional messages to consumers based on their location, essentially pushing the advertisement onto the individual. On the other hand, pull marketing involves consumers actively seeking out location-based promotions, such as using location-based apps to find nearby deals or offers. Both push and pull marketing strategies are used to target consumers based on their geographical location and encourage engagement with the brand.</p></p>
