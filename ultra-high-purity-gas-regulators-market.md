<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Dual Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultra High Purity Gas Regulators are available in two main types: Single Stage and Dual Stage. Single Stage regulators deliver a constant pressure output from the cylinder until the cylinder pressure drops to a certain level. Dual Stage regulators have two pressure reducing chambers, allowing for a more consistent performance over a wider range of inlet pressures. Single Stage regulators are simpler and more cost-effective, while Dual Stage regulators offer more precise control and stability for applications requiring a constant output pressure.</p></p>
