<p><p>Micro-RNA sequencing and assay market involves analyzing small RNA molecules for gene expression profiling. Types of sequencing technologies include sequencing by synthesis, nanopore sequencing, and ion semiconductor sequencing. Sequencing by synthesis uses fluorescently labeled nucleotides, nanopore sequencing passes DNA through protein pores for direct sequencing, and ion semiconductor sequencing measures changes in pH as bases are incorporated into DNA. Each technology offers unique advantages in terms of speed, accuracy, and cost-effectiveness for studying micro-RNAs.</p></p>
