<p><p>Phase retarders are optical components used to control the phase difference between two orthogonal polarization states of light. Copper phase retarders are made of a high-purity copper plate with a specific thickness to induce a desired phase difference. Prism phase retarders consist of a pair of identical prisms made from materials with different refractive indices, causing a phase shift between the two polarization states. Both types of phase retarders are essential in various applications such as interferometry, spectroscopy, and telecommunications.</p></p>
