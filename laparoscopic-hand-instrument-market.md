<p><p>The laparoscopic hand instrument market is divided into two main types: reusable hand instruments and disposable hand instruments. Reusable hand instruments are designed for multiple uses and are sterilized between procedures, making them cost-effective in the long run. Disposable hand instruments are designed for single-use and are discarded after each procedure, eliminating the need for sterilization and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements of healthcare professionals in the laparoscopic surgery field.</p></p>
