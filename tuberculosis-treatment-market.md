<p><p>The Tuberculosis Treatment Market can be categorized into two main types: Active TB Market and Latent TB Market. The Active TB Market focuses on treating individuals who are currently experiencing symptoms of tuberculosis and are infectious. Treatment for active TB usually involves a combination of antibiotics taken over a period of several months. On the other hand, the Latent TB Market targets individuals who have been infected with the tuberculosis bacteria but do not have active symptoms. Treatment for latent TB aims to prevent the infection from becoming active in the future.</p></p>
