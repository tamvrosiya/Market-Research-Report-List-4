<p><p>The Automotive Plastic Parts Coatings market includes three main types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. Primer is the first layer applied to the plastic part to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance. Base coat provides color and aesthetic appeal to the part, while also improving durability and UV protection. Clearcoat is the final layer that adds gloss, depth, and extra protection to the part. Each type of coating plays a vital role in enhancing the appearance and performance of automotive plastic parts.</p></p>
