<p><strong>The Two Stage Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Connection</li><li>Parallel Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the Series Connection market type, two stage pumps are connected in a linear manner where the output of the first pump serves as the input for the second pump. This configuration is suitable for applications that require high discharge pressure. On the other hand, in the Parallel Connection market type, two stage pumps are connected side by side where each pump works independently to increase the overall flow rate. This setup is commonly used in applications where high flow rates are needed.</p></p>
