Intermittent type machines are designed for batch processing, suitable for smaller workpieces, while Continuous type machines are more efficient for continuous production lines, ideal for larger workpieces. Other types of shot blasting machines in the market include hanger type, trolley type, and drum type machines, each with their own unique advantages and applications depending on the specific needs of the industry.</p></p>
