<p><p>Intermittent mist systems are commonly classified into two main types: open nozzles and closed nozzles. Open nozzles deliver mist through a continuous spray, while closed nozzles release mist in short bursts or intervals. Open nozzles are typically used for outdoor applications such as landscaping and agriculture, where a continuous mist is required. Closed nozzles, on the other hand, are often used for indoor applications like greenhouse cultivation, where precise control over misting intervals is necessary to maintain optimal growing conditions for plants.</p></p>
