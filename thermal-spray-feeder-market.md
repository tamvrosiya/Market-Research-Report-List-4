<p><p>Thermal spray feeders are used to supply materials for thermal spray processes. Wire form feeders are designed for feeding wires into the thermal spray gun, which are melted and sprayed onto surfaces. Powder form feeders, on the other hand, are used to feed powdered materials into the thermal spray gun for coating applications. Both types of feeders are essential for delivering materials effectively in thermal spray processes, with wire form feeders being suitable for wire materials and powder form feeders being suitable for powdered materials.</p></p>
