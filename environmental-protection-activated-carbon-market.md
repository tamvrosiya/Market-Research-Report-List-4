<p><p>The Environmental Protection Activated Carbon market includes three main types: Granular, Powdery, and Honeycomb. Granular activated carbon is widely used for water and air purification due to its high surface area and absorption capacity. Powdery activated carbon is more finely ground, making it beneficial for applications requiring a larger surface area per unit volume. Honeycomb activated carbon is typically used in gas purification systems due to its high mechanical strength and low pressure drop. Each type caters to specific environmental protection needs effectively.</p></p>
