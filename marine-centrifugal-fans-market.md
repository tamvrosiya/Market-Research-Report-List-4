<p><p>Marine centrifugal fans are commonly available in two market types: direct drive and belt drive. Direct drive fans have the motor directly connected to the fan impeller, offering a more compact design and lower maintenance requirements. On the other hand, belt drive fans use a belt and pulley system to transfer power from the motor to the fan, providing greater flexibility in speed adjustments and isolation of motor vibrations. Each type of fan has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
