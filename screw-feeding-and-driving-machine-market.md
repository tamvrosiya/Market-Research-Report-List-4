<p><p>Screw feeding and driving machines are divided into two main types: hand-held and automatic. These are typically used for smaller projects or in situations where mobility is important. Automatic machines, on the other hand, are fully automated and can feed, drive, and fasten screws without human intervention. These are ideal for high-volume production environments where efficiency and precision are paramount.</p></p>
