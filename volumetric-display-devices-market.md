<p><strong>The Volumetric Display Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static-volume Display</li><li>Swept-volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volumetric Display Devices come in two main types: Static-volume Display and Swept-volume Display. Static-volume Display devices create 3D images by illuminating points within a static volume, such as a hologram. Swept-volume Display devices create 3D images by projecting light onto a moving surface, such as a spinning mirror or rotating display. Both types of devices offer unique ways to display 3D content, and are used in a variety of applications including entertainment, education, and medical imaging.</p></p>
