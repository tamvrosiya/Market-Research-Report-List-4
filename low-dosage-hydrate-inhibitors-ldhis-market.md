<p><strong>The Low-Dosage Hydrate Inhibitors (LDHIs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anti-Agglomerate (AA) Inhibitors</li><li>Kinetic Hydrate Inhibitors (KHI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low-Dosage Hydrate Inhibitors (LDHIs) are chemicals used to prevent the formation of hydrates in oil and gas pipelines. Anti-Agglomerate (AA) Inhibitors work by coating hydrate crystals to prevent them from sticking together, while Kinetic Hydrate Inhibitors (KHI) work by altering the rate of hydrate formation. Both types of LDHIs are crucial in the oil and gas industry to ensure smooth operations and prevent blockages in pipelines caused by hydrates.</p></p>
